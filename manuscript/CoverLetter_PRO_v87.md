# Cover letter - Practical Radiation Oncology

Dear Editors,

We submit "Spatial heat-kernel structural priors and prescription-dose envelopes for future-lesion coverage in brain-metastasis stereotactic radiotherapy: a patient-specific RTDOSE analysis on 43 patients" for consideration as an original research article in *Practical Radiation Oncology*.

This manuscript presents a transparent retrospective stress test of future-lesion coverage in brain-metastasis stereotactic radiotherapy using the open PROTEAS-brain-mets cohort with patient-specific RTDOSE. We compare prescription-dose envelopes with a closed-form heat-kernel structural prior derived from the baseline lesion mask. The submission deliberately frames the method as an audit comparator and prospective reader-study candidate, not as a dose-escalation, treatment-modification or deployment-ready tool.

The v87 package includes a cleaned 40-reference clinical and methodological bibliography, five main figures, 11 Extended Data figures, a patient-clustered claim audit, and 300-DPI figure files. The manuscript explicitly reports that heat >=0.50 is numerically broader than dose >=95% Rx but is not a superiority endpoint because the patient-clustered mean-delta confidence interval crosses zero. The fractionation analysis is labelled exploratory because the 7-fraction stratum contains only 9 patients.

The work has not been published or submitted elsewhere. Author metadata, institutional approvals/waivers, ORCID, acknowledgements and conflict-of-interest declarations should be finalised before portal submission.
